About Me

ABOUT


My name is Leslie McCue and I am a 22 year old student from Co.Meath studying Creative Digital Media in TU Dublin. I am currently in my second year of the course. I have always loved being creative ever since I was young. When I began researching college courses a couple of years ago creative digital media really stood out to me.
Since starting the course I have gained a lot of knowledge and skills in various different outlets.


HOBBIES


In my spare time my favourite thing to do is relax and watch YouTube videos! It really helps me switch off after a long day. Since lockdown I have started playing guitar again to pass the time. I taught myself how to play the basics a few years ago but never stuck at it but thankfully I have found my love for it again.
I am also a big foodie and love trying out different receipes whenever I have some spare time.
